Marketing Analytics Work

This section highlights how I use data to evaluate audience behavior, diagnose marketing performance, and turn findings into clear recommendations.

Analytics workflow

  1. Define the marketing question.
  2. Collect data from GA4, surveys, or social platforms.
  3. Clean and structure the data for analysis.
  4. Visualize patterns in Tableau or reports.
  5. Translate insights into action.

Tools

GA4ExcelTableauRStudioBigQuery conceptsCampaign reporting

Example: Funnel-focused reporting

Instead of reporting only total traffic, I organize web performance around the user journey: acquisition, engagement, conversion action, and retention opportunity.

Recommended metrics

StageMetricDecision use
AcquisitionUsers, sessions, source/mediumIdentify traffic drivers
EngagementEngaged sessions, average engagement timeEvaluate content relevance
ConversionCTA clicks, form starts, purchasesPrioritize funnel fixes
RetentionReturning users, newsletter signupsBuild repeat audience
