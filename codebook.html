Website Traffic Analytics Codebook

Use this page as the documentation for the web traffic analytics report.

Variables

VariableDefinitionMarketing interpretation
dateEvent or session dateSupports trend analysis
source_mediumTraffic source and mediumIdentifies acquisition channel
page_titleVisited page titleShows content interest
sessionsNumber of sessionsMeasures traffic volume
engaged_sessionsSessions meeting engagement criteriaMeasures quality of visits
conversionsCompleted target eventsMeasures business outcome

Assumptions

  • GA4 tracking is implemented correctly before interpreting performance.
  • Last-click attribution is directional and may not represent the full customer journey.
  • Small sample sizes should be interpreted carefully.
