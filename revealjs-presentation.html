Website Strategy

The website is structured as a professional portfolio that connects creative execution with measurable marketing outcomes.

  • Home: profile, objective, strengths
  • Digital Marketing: original campaign/content work
  • Marketing Analytics: reporting framework
  • Website Traffic Analytics: reproducible analytics report
  • Resume: downloadable PDF

Design Philosophy

  • Warm neutral palette for a polished, approachable brand
  • Simple navigation for recruiter-friendly browsing
  • Metrics highlighted as proof points
  • Consistent cards and spacing for readability

Web Traffic Analytics Page

The analytics page demonstrates how I would evaluate traffic beyond surface-level views.

  • Acquisition: where users come from
  • Engagement: whether content holds attention
  • Conversion: whether users complete key actions
  • Recommendation: improve CTAs and validate tracking
